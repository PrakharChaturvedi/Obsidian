- Starting Lines : 
	- The "Republic of the Philippines" with it's supreme leader "Mr. Marcos" emphasizes the need for a thoughtful discussion on the deployment of Artificial Intelligence.
	
	- As the delegate of marvelous nation the "Republic of the Philippines" we entered this discussion with open minds and a desire to protect our nation's interests. Artificial Intelligence holds immense promise, but it is our duty to determine if this promise aligns with sustainability and stability for the Philippines.

- Considering the global landscape specially the WEST, it is imperative to address the diverse policies and regulations concerning AI in the Philippines and across the globe . Although our nations lacks specific legislation solely dedicated to AI, existing laws encompass overarching principles of civil law, alongside specialized statutes focused on data privacy and intellectual property, potentially applicable to AI. 

- Yet, we must collectively question: Are our current regulations robust enough to adapt to the rapidly evolving AI landscape? Do they sufficiently encapsulate the breadth of AI's potential applications and the resulting implications, especially within sectors like finance and healthcare, where AI's influence can be substantial yet potentially obscured by biases and complexities?

  - As we explore AI's potential, we must address its adverse impacts. In finance, AI-driven decisions may lack transparency and harbor biases. How do we ensure fairness and transparency, building trust on the international stage? In healthcare, dispelling misconceptions is crucial. How can we globally educate on AI's healthcare potential and limitations, fostering trust through accurate information dissemination? 

- Furthermore, the large-scale deployment of AI may exert significant environmental tolls, intensifying resource consumption. How can we globally optimize the balance between embracing AI progress and mitigating its environmental footprint? 

- Hence, it is incumbent upon every nation, including the Philippines, to contemplate the potential negative impacts of AI and implement regulations and policies to mitigate them. How can we collectively develop a framework that safeguards against the adverse effects while fostering innovation and global progress in AI?

- In conclusion, as we engage in this vital dialogue regarding the deployment of Artificial Intelligence, let us not only focus on its promises but also on its potential negative implications. By doing so, we can ensure that the development and implementation of AI on the global stage align with our shared goals of sustainability and stability. 

- Thank you, and may prosperity be upon all nations.
