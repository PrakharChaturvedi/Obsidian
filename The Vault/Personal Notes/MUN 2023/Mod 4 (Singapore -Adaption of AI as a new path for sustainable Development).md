Honorable Chair, fellow delegates 
The Philippines recognizes the importance of sustainable development in achieving our national ambition and the global goals set by the United Nations. We believe that the adoption of artificial intelligence (AI) can be a new path towards sustainable development. Here are some reasons why:

1. AI can help us preserve our planet. By using AI to analyze data, we can better understand the impact of human activities on the environment and develop solutions to mitigate them.

2. AI can help us achieve the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). The United Nations has recognized the potential of AI to drive progress across all 17 SDGs. 

3. AI can help us equip our people with the tools they need to meet the challenges of the Fourth Industrial Revolution. By investing in education and training programs, we can ensure that our citizens are prepared for the jobs of the future.

However, we must ensure that the adoption of AI is inclusive and does not leave anyone behind. We must work towards bridging the technological divide between the Global North and South.

We must also ensure that the benefits of AI are accessible to all, regardless of socioeconomic status.
In conclusion, the Philippines believes that the adoption of AI can be a new path towards sustainable development. We must work together to ensure that this path is inclusive and leads to a better future for all. Thank you.
