			- **Institutionalized Bias**: AI can institutionalize bias, since any algorithm trained on historical data will logically come to conclusions that reflect bias present in that data.
			- **AI Bias**: AI bias refers to the unintended favoritism or discrimination that can occur when AI systems are trained on biased data or reflect the biases of their developers.
			- **Promoting Undesirable Behaviors**: If an agent is promoting undesirable behaviors, its policies should be modified.
		- <font color="#ffc000">Solutions</font> 
			- **Bias Detection and Evaluation**: Implement bias detection and evaluation mechanisms to identify and assess potential biases in your AI algorithms.

- [>] Government Plans 
	- <font color="#ffc000">Philippine AI Workforce campaign</font>
		- In May 2018, The Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) together with the Department of Information and Communications Technology (DICT) and tech pioneer company AI Pros joined together to train Filipinos in using Augmented Intelligence (AI2) as a solution to the impending threat of AI on the jobs in the Information Technology-Business Process Outsourcing industry, while encouraging investors and operators to create more Socially Responsible Business Process Outsourcing (SRBPO) in the countryside.  
		- The workers will be trained and upskilled to make use of AI2-enabled systems. Augmented Intelligence will upskill the Filipino workforce and make them more effective and productive by enabling them to do complex and higher-paying tasks. 
		- This initiative will provide more jobs to Filipinos and bring the development to rural communities. DTI and DICT will introduce the presence of AI2 solution to the BPO sector in the Philippines (PH). The AI2 solution will employ impact sourcing by involving the masses in the sector’s growth. This will allow 482,000 unskilled workers to handle low-skilled work, while 525,000 low-skilled workers will perform mid-skilled work, and 309,000 mid-skilled workers will be able to do high level tasks
	- <font color="#ffc000">Open public data</font>
		- Make public data open, freely available, and downloadable in digestible format, making them ready for analysis. There are also plans to build a national data center (NDC) that will eventually be housed under the proposed National Center for AI Research (NCAIR). The NDC will be a “one-stop-shop” where datasets are stored, secured, and exchanged
	- <font color="#ffc000">Build AI research environment</font>
		- Plans to build a nurturing AI research environment so that the country can be competitive both locally and globally.
