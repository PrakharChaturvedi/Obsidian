-  Protocols : 
	- How a particular data is being sent from one computer to another is known as protocol. Their can be multiple types of protocols : TCS, IP, UDP, HTTP,  etc...
	- Speed of data transfer :
		- 1 mbps : 10,00,000 bits/s
		- 1 gbps : 10^9 bits/s
		- 1 kbps : 1000 bits/s
