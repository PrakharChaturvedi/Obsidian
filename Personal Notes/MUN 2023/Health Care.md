Honorable Chair, esteemed delegates,

The Philippines underscores the paramount importance of healthcare in achieving sustainable development. We firmly advocate for the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) to revolutionize our healthcare system, significantly improving its efficiency and impact. To this end, the Philippine government has diligently enacted policies to promote the utilization of AI within our healthcare sector, showcasing our unwavering commitment to progress and excellence.

Firstly, we have allocated significant resources to invest in AI research and development within the healthcare domain. These funds drive innovation, fostering groundbreaking solutions to elevate healthcare delivery and enhance patient outcomes nationwide.

Additionally, we have actively sought collaboration with prestigious international organizations, notably partnering with the International Telecommunication Union (ITU). This collaboration facilitates knowledge exchange, allowing us to glean insights from global best practices and foster growth within the AI-healthcare sphere.

Moreover, our government is enthusiastically encouraging the seamless integration of AI into our healthcare systems to enhance accuracy and efficiency, ultimately elevating the quality of healthcare services for our citizens.

Recognizing the critical role of a well-trained healthcare workforce, the Philippine government is investing significantly in healthcare education and training. We understand that equipping our healthcare professionals with AI-related skills is essential to unlock the potential benefits of this technology.

In conclusion, the Philippines firmly believes in the transformative power of AI in our healthcare system, and we are committed to investing and fostering its integration. Together, we can propel healthcare into a future empowered by the possibilities AI presents.

Thank you.
