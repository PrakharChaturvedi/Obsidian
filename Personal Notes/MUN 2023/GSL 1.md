*Kamusta* Po Honorable chair, vice-chair and esteemed delegates.

- As the delegate of marvelous nation the "The Republic of Philippines" we entered this discussion with open minds and a desire to protect our nation's interests.

- The Philippines, a nation deeply ingrained with the ideologies of Jose Rizal and animated by the spirits of "Pacquiao".

- Considering the global landscape specially the WEST, it is imperative to address the diverse policies and regulations concerning AI in the Philippines and across the globe . 
-  *Although our nations lacks specific legislation solely dedicated to AI.*

- We must collectively question: Are our current regulations robust enough to adapt to the rapidly evolving AI landscape? Do they sufficiently encapsulate the breadth of AI's potential applications and the resulting implications.

- Furthermore, the large-scale deployment of AI may exert significant environmental tolls, intensifying resource consumption. How Can we globally optimize the balance between embracing AI progress and mitigating its environmental footprint?

- It is incumbent upon every nation, including the Philippines, to contemplate the potential negative impacts of AI and implement regulations and policies to mitigate them. *How can we collectively develop a framework that safeguards against the adverse effects while fostering innovation and global progress in AI?*

- We can ensure that the development and implementation of AI on the global stage align with our shared goals of sustainability and stability.  

- Thank you, and may prosperity be upon all nations.
