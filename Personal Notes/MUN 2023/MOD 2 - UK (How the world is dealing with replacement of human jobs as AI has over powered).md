Esteemed chairs and delegates,   
I present the Philippine government's visionary strategy in battling AI's potential impact on our workforce. In a collaboration between the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI), the Department of Information and Communications Technology (DICT), and tech pioneer AI Pros, a pioneering initiative was launched in May 2018.

At its core is Augmented Intelligence (AI2), upskilling our workforce to handle complex tasks, mitigating job displacement. This strategy targets the Information Technology-Business Process Outsourcing (IT-BPO) sector, a linchpin of our economy. The plan promotes Socially Responsible Business Process Outsourcing (SRBPO) in rural areas, propelling localized development.
 
By infusing AI2 into the BPO sector and employing impact sourcing, this initiative anticipates reshuffling 1.3 million jobs—482,000 unskilled workers handling low-skilled roles, 525,000 advancing to mid-skilled tasks, and 309,000 engaging in high-level responsibilities.

In conclusion, the "Philippine AI Workforce" brand campaign not only protects jobs but enhances their quality. It stands as a beacon of inspiration, illustrating how AI2 can revolutionize our workforce, create jobs, and bring prosperity to underserved communities.

Thank you.
