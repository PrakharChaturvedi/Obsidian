Honorable Chair, vice chair and Esteemed delegates.

Today, we convene to deliberate upon the pressing challenges that confront our beloved nations. In our discourse, we must duly recognize the transformative potential of artificial intelligence (AI) in mitigating these predicaments. Here are some ways AI can be harnessed to *ameliorate* enhance our nation's circumstances:

1. **Disaster Preparedness and Response**: AI stands capable of forecasting natural disasters, aiding authorities in proactive readiness. Moreover, through the analysis of data gleaned from social media and diverse sources, AI can identify regions in dire need of aid during and post-disaster.
    
2. **Healthcare Advancement**: AI holds immense promise in enhancing healthcare across the Philippines. Through the analysis of patient data, it can unveil critical patterns and predict health outcomes, leading to the formulation of tailored treatment plans for individual patients.
    
3. **Efficient Traffic Management**: AI offers a solution to the endemic traffic issues that afflict our nation. By scrutinizing traffic patterns, it can guide the development of more efficient transportation systems and predict congestion, offering alternative routes.
    
In tandem with these potential solutions, we must remain vigilant regarding the ethical dimensions of AI deployment. It is imperative that we employ AI in a <font color="#ffc000">fair, transparent, and accountable manner, ensuring it neither exacerbates existing disparities nor fosters new ones.</font>

In conclusion, the transformative potential of AI to address our nation's challenges is profound. Nonetheless, a judicious and ethical approach to its utilization is paramount to ensure that all Filipinos reap its benefits. As elucidated by a recent study, generative AI could unlock an impressive *USD 79.3 billion* of productive capacity in the Philippines, equivalent to one-fifth of our GDP in 2022. This underscores the economic vistas AI can unfurl for our local industries and workforce readiness.

Thank you.
