 The Philippines recognizes the potential of artificial intelligence (AI) to drive progress across all sectors, including healthcare, education, and agriculture, among others. However, we also recognize the risks associated with the misuse of AI. As such, we believe that promoting responsibility while reducing the risk of misuse of AI is crucial. 
  -  Here are some ways we can achieve this:
 
1. Adopting principles for responsible AI. The University of the Philippines has developed a values-based approach consisting of 10 principles for responsible AI. *These principles include public good, everyone should benefit, and transparency, among others. By adopting these principles, we can ensure that the development and deployment of AI are guided by ethical considerations.*

2. Collaboration between stakeholders. The International Telecommunication Union (ITU) has recognized the importance of multi-stakeholder collaboration in the development and deployment of AI. *Collaboration between governments, academia, the private sector, and civil society can help ensure that AI is developed and deployed responsibly.**

3. Education and awareness-raising. The *European* Parliament has recognized the need for greater public  engagement and education with regard to the potential harms of AI. *By educating the public about the risks associated with the misuse of AI, we can promote responsible use of this technology.*

4. Establishing clear boundaries anchored on ethical considerations. [The Technological Institute of the Philippines has emphasized the importance of establishing clear boundaries anchored on ethical considerations when integrating AI into delivering quality education.] *By establishing clear boundaries, we can ensure that the use of AI is responsible and ethical.* 

In conclusion, the Philippines believes that promoting responsibility while reducing the risk of misuse of AI is crucial. We must adopt principles for responsible AI, collaborate between stakeholders, educate the public, and establish clear boundaries anchored on ethical considerations. By doing so, we can ensure that the development and deployment of AI are guided by ethical considerations and promote the public good.
